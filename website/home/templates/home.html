It is an honour and a source of pleasure to note that the ISTE WB section and ISTE chapters in West Bengal are consistently involved in promoting and enhancing the quality and esteem of technical education. The section and chapters routinely conduct technical events such as symposiums, seminars, technical presentations, site visits, exhibitions, etc. Some of the chapter performances are truly outstanding. The Section sponsors yearly conventions with state-level faculty chapter seminars and conferences.

The ISTE West Bengal Section encourages its member institutes to conduct faculty development programs for teachers of technical education imparting institutes/colleges. In addition to obtaining financing help from ISTE New Delhi, the institutions self-finance the courses. Through the ISTE Headquarters, the Section offers financial support to hosting institutions for the organization of Annual State Conventions for faculty and student members.
